✍️ Leadership Message

From the Principal's Desk

A warm welcome from our leadership team at Chavan-Podar Jumbo Kids, Vaijapur.

👩‍🏫
Principal

"Nurturing Curiosity, Building Character"

Dear Parents,

Welcome to Chavan-Podar Jumbo Kids, Vaijapur. Early childhood is a critical window of development, and we believe that education during these years must be relevant, developmentally appropriate, and above all, joyful.

Through our unique "Kiducation" curriculum, we focus on the holistic growth of every child—nurturing their hands (sensory coordination), their heads (critical thinking), and their hearts (emotional empathy). We do not believe in stressful grading; instead, we foster a rich environment of play-way learning where kids learn naturally through exploration.

We invite you to partner with us in this magical journey of learning, where your children can blossom into confident, caring, and lifelong learners in a 100% safe environment.

Mrs. Suvarna Kadam

Principal, Chavan-Podar Jumbo Kids (Vaijapur)

📖 Kiducation Philosophy

The Hand-Head-Heart Approach

Our curriculum is designed to stimulate the brain, promote independent thinking, and nurture emotional intelligence.

🖐️

Hand (Stimulation)

Young children learn through their senses. We stimulate cognitive development with hands-on play, sensory tables, and building blocks.

  • ✨ Sensory play (sand, water)
  • ✨ Fine & gross motor skills
  • ✨ Tactile exploration
💡

Head (Thinking)

Encourages wonder and active learning. Children are motivated to ask questions, explore nature, and solve interactive puzzles.

  • ✨ Creative problem solving
  • ✨ Number & shape concepts
  • ✨ Language & Phonics programs
❤️

Heart (Nurturing)

Emotional intelligence matters. We foster a warm environment that teaches empathy, self-help skills, cooperation, and social growth.

  • ✨ Empathy & sharing exercises
  • ✨ Self-confidence building
  • ✨ Social interaction & manners
